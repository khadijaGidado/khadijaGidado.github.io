Saffron

₦5000

Saffron is the most expensive spice of all. It has a distinctively pungent, honey-like flavor and aroma. It is available as whole threads or powdered. When ground they form a russet powder. Saffron is used to color rice dishes, sweets, puddings, sauces and soups to bright yellow.
